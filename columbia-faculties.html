Columbia Faculties

Faculty of Arts & Sciences
Director, Center for Technology Management
Dr. Langer has established an international Center for Technology Management, which encourages dialogue and collaboration among technology leaders, practitioners, students, and faculty engaged in the study of improving technology management in organizations.

School of Professional Studies
Vice Chair Faculty; Executive Director of the Division of Innovation & Design; Academic Director and Senior Lecturer in the Executive M.S. in Technology Management program; Director of M.S. in Applied Analytics and Information and Knowledge Strategy programs
Dr. Langer established a new role for faculty development and leadership for the expansion of the School of Professional Studies. He is responsible for strategy, curriculum, and recruitment for three major M.S. programs: Technology Management, Applied Analytics, and Information and Knowledge Strategy. He develops new M.S. programs using Columbia's global centers. In the Technology Management program, he created a Mentors' Program that provides students with an executive mentor from a pertinent industry; these mentors work with the students one-to-one on their Master's projects.

Teachers College
Adjunct Professor of Higher Education and Lecturer in Adult Learning & Leadership
Dr. Langer's research and instructional interests include: mentoring of nontraditional students; adult education, workplace learning, distance education, technology and education, and curricular design. The courses he developed include: IT and Organizational Learning; How Adults Learn; Purpose and Policies of Higher Education; College & University Leadership; and Technology & Higher Education.
