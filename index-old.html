Dr. Langer is the Academic Director and Faculty member of the Executive Masters in Technology Management at Columbia University. He also serves on  the faculty of Organization & Leadership at the Graduate School of Education and is the Sr. Director of the Center of Technology, Innovation, and Community Engagement at the Fu Foundation School of Engineering and Applied Science.

As a researcher, Dr. Langer has authored various papers on information systems design and management. His current research focuses on the mentoring of adult learners and the design of technology-based curricula for corporations and universities.

Dr. Langer holds a B.S. in Computer Science, an M.B.A. in Accounting/Finance, and a Doctorate of Education from Columbia University.
